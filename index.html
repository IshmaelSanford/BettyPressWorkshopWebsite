Overview

The Art of Seeing

Join us for a weekend of art, learning, and reflection at Noxubee Lodge, led by acclaimed photographer Betty Press. Set amid the prairies and woodlands of Sumter Farm, this immersive workshop explores the art of seeing through the lens — whether with a professional camera or the one you carry every day.

Participants will learn to capture natural light, texture, and mood; experiment with composition; and create an emulsion lift artwork to take home. This unique event blends field sessions, indoor creative time, and moments of quiet inspiration within Alabama's most beautiful prairie landscape.

  • 01 Field Sessions Early morning and late afternoon walks across Wild Horse, Betty and Bob, and #2 Prairie
  • 02 Emulsion Lift Hands-on session to create a unique photographic artwork to take home
  • 03 Conversation Fireside discussions on art, land, and stewardship after dinner
